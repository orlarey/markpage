# Columns — side-by-side layout

A `::: columns` fenced div splits its content at each `---` into equal
columns. Two `---` give three columns.

::: columns
**Before**

- slow
- verbose
- manual

---

**After**

- fast
- concise
- automatic
:::

Three columns:

::: columns
Column one with a short paragraph.

---

Column two, also short.

---

Column three closes the row.
:::
